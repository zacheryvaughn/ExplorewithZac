WHO AM I

ZAC VAUGHN



My name is Zac and I'm a 25 year old from the United States living in Bali. 3 years ago, I took the opportunity to teach english online. This job allowed me to move to Bali and paid more than enough to thrive here. But somehow I found myself depressed and drained, even in this tropical paradise. In that crucial time, I learned the most important lesson of my life. I discovered that no amount of money or success would make me happy if I wasn't doing what I loved. So, I quit.


I decided to cut my own path by starting a YouTube channel to share my journey and inspire others. I spent the next 3 years diving deep into the creative process. I learned a lot and had a lot of early success, though, not without a fair amount of difficulties; creating content as a traveler during the pandemic was a struggle. But I'm back, and my mission is to provide viewers with the utmost value regarding Bali travel and the digital nomad lifestyle.
